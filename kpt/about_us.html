About Our Company

Experienced in Purchasing Troubled Assets

For over 10 Years, we have been providing professional services, dealing with liquidation and closeout merchandise. We buy a wide variety of general consumer merchandise, electronics, toys, beauty products, gifts, housewares, novelties, etc.Quantities vary and we will consider an inventory of any size.

Expert in Inventory Solutions, Asset Recovery, Liquidations

We have the ability to make a deal within 24 hours of hearing from you. Normal channels of distribution are always protected for the manufacturers, importers, distributors, retailers, and service companies that deal directly with us.

We will discuss with you, the best possible solutions for getting rid of unwanted inventory.

Export

We are active in the export of merchandise all over the world. Our extensive network of freight companies allows us to get the best possible freight costs in order to pass the savings on to our customers.

Location

Our main office and warehouse is located in Markham, Ontario. We also have a partner office in Birmingham, United Kingdom.

Customer Satisfaction & Protection

We are committed to providing a safe trading environment for our customers. All transactions are documented carefully and undertaken procedurally following all guidelines in accordance with Canadian Government Laws.

Logistics

Orders are usually shipped from our main warehouse in Markham unless otherwise specified. We are able to ship using

  • Ocean Containers (40ft and 20ft)
  • Trailers (48ft and 53ft)
  • LTL
  • Loose Pallets

Finance & Payments

Buying from us is very simple and we offer many payment methods to accomodate customer confidence. We accept Wire Transfers, L/C, T/T, Bank Drafts, Certified Cheques,Credit Cards and Cash. Selling to us is equally as simple. We pay prior to pickup of inventory.
